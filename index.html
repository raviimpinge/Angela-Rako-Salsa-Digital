Carbon footprint

Considerably reduce your carbon footprint. Mother earth, it’s our pleasure.

Generating a static version of your site means your Drupal host only needs computing power to serve your content editors^¹, greatly reducing your hosting and carbon footprint. Better yet, we can put your Drupal servers to sleep when not in use by content editors, saving even more power and costs.

^1 Your static public content will still require power, though significantly less.

Security

Significantly reduce
cyber threats. Hackers beware.

Lockdown Drupal and restrict access to content editors only. It’s a one-way street from here allowing updates to be pushed to your site while also denying any unwanted access from the outside.

^2 When converting your website to 100% static.
